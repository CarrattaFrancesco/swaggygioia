I'm Gioia Maria Carratta, a multidisciplinary designer based in Milan — cool kid, apparently — and currently studying Creative Direction at NABA. My work moves between graphic design, art direction, editorial design and creative strategy, often expanding into UX/UI, motion and video when the project calls for it.

I collaborate on visual identities, campaigns, merchandise and cultural projects, exploring how design can connect ideas, people and everyday experiences. Along the way I also write, research and occasionally illustrate when necessary.
